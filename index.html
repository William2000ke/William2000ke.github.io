Professional Summary

A finance professional built for precision.

Detail-oriented Finance and Accounting professional with 2+ years of progressive experience across medical supplies and the public sector. My career spans accounts payable/receivable management, bank reconciliations, financial reporting, audit support, and budgeting — each role sharpening my ability to deliver accurate, compliant, and insightful financial work.

Currently serving as Assistant Accountant at Frontcare Medical Supplies, where I process 500+ supplier and client invoices monthly and have achieved 100% bank reconciliation accuracy. I bring strong IFRS compliance knowledge and a deep understanding of Kenyan tax regulations including VAT, PAYE, NSSF, and NHIF.
